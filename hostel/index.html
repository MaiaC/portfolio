CoolTour Hostel

Budapest, Hungary

CoolTour Hostel is located in the heart of Budapest, a short walk from Deak Square and the well known Kiraly Street. Here, you can find everything unique to Budapest; famous ruin bars, excellent street food and alternative local businesses.


Our English speaking staff make it easy to meet other cool travelers, being experienced back packers themselves. Having lived in Budapest, they are knowledgeable about the local area, allowing them to make excellent recommendations. CoolTour Hostel also offers a vast array of activities appealing to both party-goers and holiday makers alike.


Located beneath the hostel is The CoolTour bar, offering great entertainment and cheap drink specials weekly, exclusively to our guests.


Cleanliness is of high priority here at CoolTour Hostel. We are committed to keeping our facilities, kitchen, common area and dorms to an exceptional standard. In addition to this, all beds have an assigned locker for personal belongings and padlocks are also available at reception.


Cooltour Hostel Policies & Conditions:

  • Cancellation policy: 48 hours before arrival.
  • Check in from 11:00 untill 14:00 pm.
  • Check out before 10:00
  • Payment upon arrival by cash.
  • Taxes included.
  • Breakfast not included.
  • No curfew.
  • No smoking.

We take 6000 HUF or 20 EUR per person as a deposit from guests arriving in group of more than 5 guests.


The reception is open until 14 PM and reopens at 09 AM. Allowances can be made if notification is given.


There is no late check in option for the guests who book only 1 night.


Reservations made after 14 PM on arrival date acceptable only confirmation in advance - please contact Hostel reception.
